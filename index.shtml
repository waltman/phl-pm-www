Meetings   Technical Talks
We generally meet bi-weekly. In a four week month, we'd meet once for a technical presentation and discussion, and once for drinking, eating, and social discussion. Our technical meetings are usually held in the second floor conference room at Thomson Scientific (formerly ISI), 3501 Market Street in Philadelphia; the location for ``social'' meetings changes monthly. The Reading Groups have been held at Marathon Grill, 16th and Sansom

Our typical meeting schedule is:
  • First Monday, 7:00 PM--whenever: eating, drinking, and discussion.
  • Third Monday, 7:00 PM--whenever: Technical Talk
 Once a month, we meet and someone gives a presentation about technical issues about Perl, or the world surrounding Perl. Past talks include XML, Perl/Tk, Perl in the real world, Regular Expressions, and more.

Generally our speakers come from within phl.pm, but from time to time we bring in a guru from elsewhere in the Perl community.

An archive of past talks is available.

Mailing Lists   Other Stuff
We have a rather low-volume mailing list, used mostly for negotiation of meeting locations and occasional technical chatter. To subscribe, send email to majordomo@lists.pm.org with a message body of ``subscribe phl''.
